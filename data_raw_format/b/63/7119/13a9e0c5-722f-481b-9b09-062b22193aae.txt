Mita Patel
Research Support Administrator
Email: edupgr@le.ac.uk
Mita’s role covers a variety of duties surrounding the School of Education’s research administration. Mita supports internal and external funding, develops and coordinates information systems relating to research and assists in the School’s submission for the Research Excellence Framework (REF).
Mita also provides administrative support for the School of Education Research Committee and helps facilitate the presentation and sharing of research. Her role includes calculating costings for funding applications.
