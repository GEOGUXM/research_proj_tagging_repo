Jean-Philippe P. Richard, Ph.D.
Ph.D. Georgia Institute of Technology, 2002
Jean-Philippe P. Richard is a professor of industrial and systems engineering at the University of Florida. His research and teaching focuses on theoretical and computational aspects of mixed integer linear and nonlinear programming, and the applications of these techniques in transportation, logistics and data.
