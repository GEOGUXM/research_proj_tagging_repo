Ms Agnes Michalik BSc MSc MIScT RSci
Geochemistry Research Technician
Related links
Ms Agnes Michalik is a Geochemistry Research Technician in Ocean and Earth Science, National Oceanography Centre Southampton at the University of Southampton.
PhD researcher - University of Southampton (2014 - present)
BSc Chemical Engineering - Military University of Technology in Warsaw, Poland (2004)
MSc Oceanology - University of Gdańsk, Poland (2003)
Research
Responsibilities
Publications
Contact
Research interests
Isotope geochemistry; Forensic analysis of hip implant failure.
Research project: Metals, Environment and Health
Objective of the study is to perform a series of elemental analyses of biological materials that can help in the design of prostheses and surgical procedures and minimise potentially adverse health impacts from breakdown of implants.
Research group
