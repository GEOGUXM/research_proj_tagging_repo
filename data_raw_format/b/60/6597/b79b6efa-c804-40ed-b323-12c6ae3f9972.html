profile


Dr. Zhong Su is a Research Staff Member at the IBM Research - China and manages the Information Analysis and Search team. He joined CRL after receiving his Ph.D. degree in computer science at Tsinghua University in 2002. He has been involved in many projects in CRL including text analytics, enterprise search, metadata management and information integration.

Dr. Su leads the Chinese Knowledge Management and Natural Language Processing project teams, which were awarded the Technical Accomplishment of IBM research in 2005,2006,2007 and Outstanding Technical Accomplishment of IBM research in 2008. Dr. Su is IBM Master Inventor.
