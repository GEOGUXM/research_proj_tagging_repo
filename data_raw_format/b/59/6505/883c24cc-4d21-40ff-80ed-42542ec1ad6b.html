In 2017, ECU has been ranked in the world's top 150 universities under 50 years old.

The Good Universities Guide has given us a 5-star rating for teaching quality for 8 years in a row.

That's how university should be.

Proud to be a member of the Athena SWAN Charter in Australia, a program focused on improving the recruitment, retention and promotion of women in science, technology, engineering, maths and medicine.

ECU is proud to be leading the way in workplace gender equality. The Federal Workplace Gender Equality Agency's citation recognises our leadership in this area.

ECU has received the highest student satisfaction rating of all Australian public universities in the 2017 Australian Quality Indicators for Learning and Teaching survey.
