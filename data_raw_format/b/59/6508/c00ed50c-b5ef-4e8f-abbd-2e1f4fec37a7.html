Double Success in the Complete University Guide 2017

The School of Medicine, Dentistry & Nursing is delighted to announce that both Dentistry and Nursing have been ranked #1 in UK in the 2017 Complete University Guide. Both subjects have achieved 100% for the second consecutive year. Medicine remains in the top 10, with a high overall score of 96.8%.
