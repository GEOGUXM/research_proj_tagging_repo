Dr. Dave R. Wilson

David R. Wilson
Professor, Orthopaedics (Associate Member, Mechanical Engineering) B.Eng. (McGill) D.Phil.(Oxon)

Ph: 604-675-2584

Email: david.wilson@ubc.ca

Site: Centre for Hip Health and Mobility at the Vancouver General Hospital (www.hiphealth.ca)

Research Interests

• Hip, knee and spine mechanics
• Causes and treatments of osteoarthritis
• Medical imaging and medical image processing
• Orthopaedic sports medicine

Selected Current Projects

• Evaluation of the link between the mechanical changes produced by high tibial osteotomy and cartilage health
• Development of new methods for measuring cartilage strain in the loaded hip joint using the Canada Light Source synchrotron
• Application of open MRI to visualize impingement between the femur and acetabulum in patients with femoroacetabular impingement (FAI)
• Development of a system for navigated resection of bone deformities associated with femoroacetabular impingement
• Exploration of links between bone architecture, joint mechanics, and cartilage degeneration
