Cindy Grimm

Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering

Robotics

Office: 
311 Graf Hall
Phone:

Fax:

Email:

PhD, Computer Science, Brown University, 1996
MA, Computer Science, Brown University, 1992
BA, Computer Science, University of California at Berkeley, 1990
BA, Art, University of California at Berkeley, 1990

At OSU since 2012.

Associate Professor, Washington University in St. Louis, 2007–12.
Assistant Professor, Washington University in St. Louis, 2000–07.
Postdoctoral Researcher, Brown University, 1999–2000.
Postdoctoral Researcher, Microsoft Research, 1996–99.
Lecturer, Brown University, 1995.

Research Interests: 

Dr. Grimm is currently working in the areas of computer graphics and human–computer interaction, including the relationship between shape and biological function, design of interfaces for manipulating and annotating 3D images, and artistic-based modeling and visualization.
