Biography
Sarah joined the School of Economics in October 2000 as a Research Fellow in CFCM. She was promoted to Associate Professor in August 2014. Her research interests lie in the areas of applied microeconometrics and labour economics.
Sarah's CV can be downloaded here in pdf format. Sarah's Facewall page.
