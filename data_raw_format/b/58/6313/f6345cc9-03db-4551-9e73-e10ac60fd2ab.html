Research Interests

Dr. Lawrence's research interests include Valerius Maximus, exemplarity, Roman identity, Roman Stoicism and Classics pedagogy.

Selection of Recent Research

2014 Latins, Italians, Romans, idem sanguis? ASCS 35, Massey University

2013 How to be Good: The virtues of the non-elite in Seneca's Epistulae Morales, Classics and Ancient History Seminar Series, University of Sydney

2013 How to be Good: The language of virtue in Valerius Maximus, School of Humanities Seminar Series, UNE

2013 Dead on Time: Valerius Maximus and cupiditas vitae, ASCS 34, Macquarie University

2006 Inside Out: the Depiction of Externality in Valerius Maximus (PhD; currently under revision for publication as monograph)

Research Supervision Experience

Dr. Lawrence has supervised and examined research projects at the Masters and Honours level. She has also been involved in promoting undergraduate research at UNE as one of the organisers of the Inaugural UNE Undergraduate Research Summer School (2013) and is able to supervise HDR projects in Latin language and Roman History.
