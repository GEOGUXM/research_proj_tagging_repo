Idlan Zakaria is a lecturer in accounting and currently the Programme Director for the BSc Accounting and Finance (Singapore). She teaches on financial accounting modules.

Open all sections

Qualifications

  • PhD Accounting and Finance, Lancaster University (2008)
  • MA Accounting and Finance, Lancaster University (2001)
  • B. Commerce (Hons), Lincoln University, New Zealand (1999)

Biography

Prior to joining the University of Birmingham in January 2016, Idlan was a lecturer in accounting at Essex Business School, University of Essex from 2006-2015. She has also been a visiting lecturer at Universiti Putra Malaysia in 2009. She has experience in teaching and research (including supervision) in financial accounting and corporate governance at all levels.

Teaching

  • Introduction to Financial Reporting
  • Financial Reporting
  • Advanced Financial Accounting

Research

  • Corporate governance (board structure, executive pay, international corporate governance, alternative corporate governance structures)
  • Financial reporting (disclosure, earnings management, culture)

Other activities
