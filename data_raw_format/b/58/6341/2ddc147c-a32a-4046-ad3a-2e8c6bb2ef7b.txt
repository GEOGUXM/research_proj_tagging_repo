Alessandro Rinaldo
I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from Carnegie Mellon under the supervision of Stephen Fienberg in 2005. I joined the Statistics Department as visiting faculty in 2005 and, in 2007, became a tenure track faculty.
Lab Groups

Specific Research Interests
My research interests include: discrete data models, networks models, geometric and topological data analysis, algebraic statistics, high-dimensional inference, structured normal means problems. log-linear models and models for discrete data
