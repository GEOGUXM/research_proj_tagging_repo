Petar Momcilovic

Ph.D. Columbia University, 2003

Petar Momcilovic serves as associate professor and master’s program coordinator of industrial and systems engineering at the University of Florida.  His teaching and research interests include stochastic modeling with a focus on queueing theory and analysis of algorithms. He is interested in various business and technological applications that include service systems, hospital operations and communication networks.
