Dan Nathanson

Professor (Restorative Sciences, Biomaterials, MSE), Chairman (Restorative Sciences, Biomaterials)

  • Office 780 Harrison Avenue
  • Email dnathan@bu.edu
  • Phone 617-638-4702

Professor (Restorative Sciences, Biomaterials, MSE), Chairman (Restorative Sciences, Biomaterials)

  • Primary Appointment Restorative Sciences/Biomaterials
  • Additional Affiliations Materials Science and Engineering
  • Areas of Interest Biomaterials with emphasis is on esthetic restorative materials.
  • Research Areas Biomaterials. Research emphasis is on esthetic restorative materials. Research projects include clinical and animal studies, as well as in vitro research spanning topics from metals to composites and ceramics, biocompatibility, machined restorations and restoration of pulpless teeth.
