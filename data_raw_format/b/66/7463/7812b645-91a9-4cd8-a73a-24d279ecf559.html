Dr. Joseph Webb is an assistant professor of anesthesiology in the Department of Anesthesiology at Emory University Hospital. His clinical expertise includes neurovascular, vascular and liver transplant anesthesiology.

Medical School: The Medical College of Georgia, Augusta, GA

Internship: Emory University School of Medicine

Residency: Anesthesiology, Emory University School of Medicine, Atlanta, GA

Fellowship: Neurovascular Anesthesiology, Emory University School of Medicine, Atlanta, GA

Board Certification: American Board of Anesthesiology
