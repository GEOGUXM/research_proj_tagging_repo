Tracey L. O‘Connor MD

Department of Medicine

Clinical Assistant Professor

Specialty/Research Focus

Oncology

 
Overview Research Activities Latest News

Education and Training:
  • Fellowship, Medical Oncology, Roswell Park Cancer Institute (2002)
  • Residency, Internal Medicine, Strong Memorial Hospital, U. of Rochester Medical Center (2000)
  • MD, Internal Medicine, Oncology, University at Buffalo, The State University of New York (1997)
Employment:
  • Associate Professor of Oncology, Department of Medicine, Roswell Park Cancer Institute (2012-present)
  • Assistant Professor of Oncology, Department of Medicine, Roswell Park Cancer Institute (2002–2012)
Awards and Honors:
  • Health Care 50 Winner, 2012 (2012)
  • The Arnold P. Gold Foundation for Humanism in Medicine Faculty Award (2010)
  • Buffalo Business First "40 Under 40" Award (2009)
  • ASCO/AACR Methods in Clinical Cancer Research Protocol (2001)
  • Commendation from Dept. of Medicine Director, Mortality & Morbidity Conf. (2000)
  • Alpha Omega Alpha Hatem Research Fellowship Commendation by Dean
