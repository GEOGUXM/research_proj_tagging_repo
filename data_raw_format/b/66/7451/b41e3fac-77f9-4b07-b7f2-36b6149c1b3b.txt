DEBORAH ROWE
SENIOR RESEARCH OFFICER
Deborah Rowe is a member of the Monash Architecture Studio (MAS).

Contact Deborah
With MAS, she works with government bodies and academic partners to develop more affordable, sustainable housing and urban precinct models.
Deborah has a background in Visual Culture, and prior to first joining MAS in 2010, Deborah worked at a range of offices in Melbourne and Berlin including Ashton Raggatt McDougall, Edmond & Corrigan and Hackenbroich.
