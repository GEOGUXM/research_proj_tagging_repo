Introduction
Andrew Gillespie is a Professor of Communications Geography within the Centre for Urban and Regional Development Studies (CURDS) in the School of Geography, Politics and Sociology (GPS). He was the Acting Research Dean for the HaSS Faculty between April 2013 and September 2014, and was previously the Head of School of GPS between 2005 and 2013 and the Executive Director of CURDS between 1994 and 2003.
