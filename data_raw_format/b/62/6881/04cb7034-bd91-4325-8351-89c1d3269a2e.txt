Griffin Jardine, MD, specializes in pediatric eye diseases as well as adult strabismus. He offers medical and surgical care in these fields with a special emphasis in the treatment of amblyopia, strabismus, pediatric glaucoma, anterior segment disorders, pediatric cataracts, retinopathy of prematurity and nasolacrimal duct obstruction.
