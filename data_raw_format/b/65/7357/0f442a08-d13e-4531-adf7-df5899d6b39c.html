Dr Robert Strzepek

Fellow
Researcher - Marine Biogeochemistry
Jaeger 6, Rm J6.216
 robert.strzepek@anu.edu.au
 +61 2 61258322
  • Profile
  • Research

Profile

Qualifications

BSc Marine Biology, McGill University, Montréal, Canada; PhD Botany, University of British Columbia, Vancouver, Canada

Research

Research interests

Marine biogeochemical cycles of bioactive metals; biological oceanography; physiological ecology and evolution of marine phytoplankton, particularly species indigenous to the Southern Ocean; adaptations of phytoplankton to low iron availability, specifically the effects of iron availability on photosynthetic physiology and photoacclimatory strategies; marine genomics and proteomics.
