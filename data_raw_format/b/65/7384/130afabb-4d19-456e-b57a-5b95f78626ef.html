                                                              Undergraduate Admission

                                                              Are you in the next generation of social transformers? We thought so. To join one of our programs, begin by completing ASUs undergraduate admission application.

                                                              Once youre admitted, well see you at orientation to help you pick courses for your first semester and answer any questions you might have.

                                                              Undergraduate Admission  |  Tuition Information  |  Scholarships & Awards 

                                                              Graduate Admission

                                                              Take the next step in your academic career. When you start a masters or PhD program at the School of Social Transformation, youll be preparing for a career dedicated to social justice.

                                                              Each program at SST has different admissions requirements and deadlines.
