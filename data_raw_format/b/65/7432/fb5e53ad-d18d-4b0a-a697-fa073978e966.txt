AboutPublications
Helen Land commenced work as a Clinical Educator at the JCU Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy Clinic in January 2008 after operating a successful Private Practice in Townsville for 20 years. Her area of interest is Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy.
Helen  is currently the Director of the JCU Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy Clinic, which involves managing the overall running of the clinic in addition to her clinical education role. She also coordinates two post-graduate Masters subjects 1. PS5109 Ergonomics and 2. PS5108 Advanced Musculoskeletal Practice.
She is both a member of the Australian Physiotherapy Association and Sports Medicine Australia.
Helen is now in the final stages of her PhD titled ‘Physiotherapy Management of Shoulder Impingement:'.
Helen supervises Physiotherapy Honours students in areas of musculoskeletal physiotherapy.
