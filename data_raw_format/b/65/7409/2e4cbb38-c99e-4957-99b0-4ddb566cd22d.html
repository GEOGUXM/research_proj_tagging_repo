Research Interests: 
television studies, race and media, global media

Tim’s research and teaching interests include television studies; media globalization; race, ethnicity, and media; and critical analysis of media industries. He is the author of Black Television Travels: Media Globalization and Contemporary Racial Discourse (NYU Press, 2013) and Global Television Marketplace (BFI Publishing, 2006); the co-author with Amanda D. Lotz of Understanding Media Industries (Oxford University Press, 2011); and a former Senior Fulbright Scholar to Hungary. His research has also appeared in Communication, Culture & Critique; Critical Studies in Media Communication; The Journal of Broadcasting & Electronic Media; International Communication Gazette; and Global Media Journal; as well as in several anthologies on media globalization and television studies.
