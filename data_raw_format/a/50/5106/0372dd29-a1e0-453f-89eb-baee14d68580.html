Co-Director, Center for Program Design and Evaluation at Dartmouth

Department(s):
Community and Family Medicine

Education:
Luther College, BA 1989
U. Connecticut, MA 1993
U. Connecticut, PHD 2000
Dartmouth College, MPH 2011

Programs:
The Dartmouth Institute for Health Policy and Clinical Practice

Websites:
http://geiselmed.dartmouth.edu/cpde/

Center for Program Design and Evaluation

Qualitative and mixed methods research
Evaluation

Technology in education
