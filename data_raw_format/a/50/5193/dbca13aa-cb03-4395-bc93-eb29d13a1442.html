T. Drew Coker

T. Drew Coker, M.S., R.D., C.S.S.D., C.S.C.S. is a 2009 graduate of Madonna University with a bachelor’s degree in dietetics. He completed his dietetic internship at Michigan State University(MSU) and became a registered dietitian in 2010. That same year he began working as a clinical dietitian at Oakwood Hospital and Medical Center in the acute care setting covering a variety of specialty units. In 2011, Drew started working with Spartan Performance as a graduate assistant and throughout that time completed his Master’s degree in Kinesiology specializing in exercise physiology at Michigan State University in 2013. As a member of the MSU Health Team, Drew performs nutrition counseling for MSU athletes and the community. Drew is a Certified Specialist in Strength and Conditioning and is also a Certified Specialist in Sports Dietetics. Drew loves the relationship that nutrition and exercise have with each other and as former college basketball player tries to stay in shape and keep up his basketball skills as much as possible.
