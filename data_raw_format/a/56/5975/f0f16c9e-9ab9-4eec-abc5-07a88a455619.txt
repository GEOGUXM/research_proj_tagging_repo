About me
My role in the School of Pharmacy & Medical Sciences is to manage and lead development of our research efforts, and governance of  our research degree programs. I also support the Head of School with respect to overall operations and strategic initiatives.
For my own research I lead the Healthy Environments, Healthy People Research Group, and am part of the Centre for Population Health Research.
My research interests include medical entomology, the epidemiology of infectious diseases, ecology and evolution.
