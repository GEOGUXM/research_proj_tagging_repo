Dr. Jessica R. Berman specializes in the treatment of systemic lupus erythematosus, rheumatoid arthritis, and mixed and undifferentiated connective tissue diseases. Throughout her career, she has published numerous papers and has been heavily involved in lupus research. Dr. Berman is an Assistant Attending Rheumatologist at Hospital for Special Surgery.
