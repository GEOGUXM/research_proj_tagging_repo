Plant Agriculture

Canada’s largest and most diverse applied plant biology department. We are a research intensive department within the Ontario Agricultural College dedicated to teaching, research and service related to horticultural crops, turfgrass, landscape species and field crops. We provide hands-on learning opportunities from nationally and internationally recognized experts to students at the diploma, undergraduate and graduate levels.
