Clinical Interest
Dr. Abbasi is a clinical endocrinologist who practices comprehensive adult endocrinology with particular interest in diabetes, thyroid, pituitary and bone disorders. She provides care at outpatient clinic as well as inpatient hospital endocrinology consultation.
Educational Interest
Dr. Abbasi is interested and involved in medical students and residents teaching activities.
