            Research Interest
Wheat establishment under fluctuating environmental conditions: The highly variable and unpredicted rainfall within and between seasons, which characterize the Mediterranean region, results in significant wheat yield losses. Our study focuses on improving wheat growth under fluctuating environmental conditions.
Cereals adaptation to environmental stress combinations: Under field conditions, plants routinely subjected to combinations of environmental stresses. Plant response(s) to stress combinations differ from the response(s) to individual stress condition. We use the model grass Brachypodium to study genes and gene networks involved in plants adaptation to multiple environmental stresses.
Herbicides resistant in grasses weeds: Lolium rigidum Gaudin (annual ryegrass), is a major grass weed infesting cereal crops in Israel. In recent years, L. rigidum resistant to ACCase, ALS and EPSPS inhibitors is spreading worldwide. Our research aims to elucidate the mechanisms of resistant involves in resistant populations.
