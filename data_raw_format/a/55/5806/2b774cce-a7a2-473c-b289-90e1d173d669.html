Clinical Interest
E. Raymund Ramirez, MD joined the Division of General Internal Medicine at the University of Pittsburgh in 2013. Dr. Ramirez is currently both a nighttime hospitalist in the Section of Hospital Medicine serving as a faculty preceptor for internal medicine residents completing their night float rotation, and working as a daytime Academic Hospitalist on the Medicine teaching teams with Housestaff. Dr. Ramirez is also part of the orthopedic co-management team.
Educational Interest
Along with working with residents, interns and medical students on the housestaff teams, Dr. Ramirez presents didactic lectures for our Junior Hospitalist Residents.
