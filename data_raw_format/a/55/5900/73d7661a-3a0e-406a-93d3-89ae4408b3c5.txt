Dr Arben Kita PhD, MSc, BSc
Lecturer in Finance
Related links
  Arben Kita has been a Lecturer in Finance at Southampton Business School since 2014. Prior to joining the University of Southampton, Arben lectured in the Department of Economics, Econometrics and Finance, at the University of Groningen in the Netherlands.
Arben has a PhD in Finance from University of Bangor, MSc from Bocconi University and BSc from University of Bologna.

Research interests
Arben’s research focuses on empirical asset pricing, empirical analysis of fixed income and derivative pricing models, behavioural asset pricing, and market microstructure.
Research group
Centre for Computational Finance and Business Analytics (CCFBA)
