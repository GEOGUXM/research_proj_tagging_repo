Jo Mitchell

Trial Co-ordinator

Email: jm294@medschl.cam.ac.uk

Tel: 01223 761760

Background

Jo is a trial coordinator in the Behaviour Science Group currently working on the iQuit in Practice study. She has been involved in a number of studies led by the department in many varied roles (field work and coordination) and  most recently was the trial coordinator for work-stream 4 of the Very brief Interventions (VBI) programme.
