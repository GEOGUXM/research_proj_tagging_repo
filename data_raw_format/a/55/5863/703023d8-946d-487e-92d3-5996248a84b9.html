Bio

Jeffrey T. Macher is a Professor of Strategy, Economics and Policy in the McDonough School of Business at Georgetown University. He is also the Academic Director of Georgetown’s Center for Business and Public Policy.

He teaches full-time, evening and executive MBA and executive education courses in microeconomics, competitive strategy, and the management of technology and innovation.

Prior to Georgetown University, Professor Macher worked for Braxton Associate (a strategy consulting firm now part of Deloitte Consulting), IBM and Motorola.

Professor Macher is a member of the Academy of Management (AOM), the American Economics Association (AEA), the American Society of Health Economists (ASHEcon), the Society for Institutional and Organizational Economics (SIOE) and the Strategic Management Society (SMS). He is on the editorial boards of Production and Operations Management Journal and Strategic Management Journal, and a reviewer for several academic journals. He is also a Special Government Employee (SGE) of the Food and Drug Administration (FDA).
