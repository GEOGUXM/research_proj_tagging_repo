Salvador Espinosa, Ph.D.

Education

Masters and a Ph.D. in Public Affairs from Indiana University
B.A. in Economics from Universidad Panamericana (Mexico)

Office Hours

Mondays 2:00-4:00pm and by appt.

Office: PSFA 173

E-mail: salvador.espinosa@sdsu.edu

Phone: 619-594-5880

Salvador Espinosa is a scholar and public finance consultant specializing in fiscal decentralization, public financial administration and regional development. He holds a Masters and a Ph.D. in Public Affairs from Indiana University, as well as a B.A. in Economics from Universidad Panamericana (Mexico). His current research focuses on the budgetary impact of federal transfers in Mexico. Before starting his doctoral degree, Salvador worked as Budget Analyst for the City of Bloomington, Indiana; as Research Associate for the Indiana Fiscal Policy Institute (Indianapolis, Indiana) and Coordinator of Information and Economic Development for the government of the state of Guanajuato (Mexico).
For more information please visit:
