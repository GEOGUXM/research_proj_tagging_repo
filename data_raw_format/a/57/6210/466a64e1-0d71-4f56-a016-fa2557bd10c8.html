Research

Research interests

  • Electron microscopy, diffraction and microanalysis
  • Microstructures
  • Identification of processes responsible for deformation and seismic-wave attenuation grain boundaries
  • Melt distribution at the grain scale in partially molten rocks
  • Microstructural changes for mineral reactions with large volume change
  • Nanotubes - C and BN
  • Defects in semiconductors following ion implantation and annealing

Projects

Supervisor

  • Deformation in zircon
