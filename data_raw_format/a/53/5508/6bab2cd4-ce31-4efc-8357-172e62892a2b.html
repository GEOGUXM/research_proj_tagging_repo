Advancement & Community Relations

The Offices of Advancement and Community Relations are dedicated to forwarding education, healthcare, research and community service endeavors of the University of Illinois College of Medicine at Peoria.

To learn more about UICOMP's giving opportunities, publications, and community programs, please call 309-671-8404, 309-680-8613, or 309-671-8481. 

And don't forget to visit UICOMP on FACEBOOK .
