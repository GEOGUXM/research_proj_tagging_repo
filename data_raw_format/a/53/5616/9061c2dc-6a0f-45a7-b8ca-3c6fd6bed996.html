      Research

      Research Interests


      • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), sustainability and business ethics
      • International management 
      • Global governance 
      • Adoption of organisational practices in multinational enterprises (MNEs)
      • Translation of institutional pressures
      • Standardization of CSR
      I am currently working on projects in three areas of research: (1) the influence of the new global governance infrastructure (through CSR standards) on MNEs’ management practices; (2) the translation of complex institutional pressures in different offices of MNEs; (3) the integration of CSR practices inside MNEs, with a particular focus on the parent-subsidiary relationships.
