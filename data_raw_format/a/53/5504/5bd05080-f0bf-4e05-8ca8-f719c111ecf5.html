Profile

Qualifications

PhD (Igneous Petrology), ANU 2014; BSc (Geology) (Hons), ANU 2009; BEng (Chem) (Hons)/BSc (Geosciences/Chemistry) – Monash University 2008

Biography

I teach a wide variety of courses at undergraduate and masters level within RSES, including Groundwater, Igneous Petrology and Earth Systems Science. I am alos responsible for the teaching facilities including petrographic microcsopes and teaching rock and thin section collections.
