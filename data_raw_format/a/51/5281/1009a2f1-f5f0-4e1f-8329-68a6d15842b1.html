    Professor

    Nicolas Hadjiconstantinou

    Professor; Co-Director, Computation for Design and Optimization; Co-Director, Computational Science and Engineering

    Interests

    1. Kinetic transport for small-scale fluid flow and solid-state heat transfer applications
    2. Molecular and stochastic simulation of nanoscale transport phenomena
    3. Molecular and multiscale simulation method development
