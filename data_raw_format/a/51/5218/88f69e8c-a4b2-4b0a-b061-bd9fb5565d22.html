David B. Bogy

William S. Floyd, Jr., Distinguished Chair Director, Computer Mechanics Laboratory Professor of Mechanical Engineering

6103 Etcheverry Hall, Mailstop 1740
University of California at Berkeley
Berkeley, CA 94720-1740
(510) 642-2570

 

Email: dbogy@berkeley.edu

 

Research

Mechanics in computer technology: tribology in hard-disk drives, laser measurement systems, numerical simulations. Static and dynamic problems in solid and fluid mechanics.

 

For more information on Professor Bogy's research, please visit the Computer Mechanics Lab website.
