Kathleen Hickey, a native Chicagoan, is a choreographer, dancer and teacher. Currently based in West Lafayette, Indiana, her work has been seen throughout Chicago, Milwaukee, New York, and the Indianapolis region. She has performed in works by various artists at the New York Drama League, in New York City, Mitchell Hall in Milwaukee, WI, and at the Kampa Museum, in Prague in the Czech Republic. Hickey holds a B.A. in Political Science from Purdue University and an M.F.A. in Dance and Performing Arts from the University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee and currently is a Visiting Instructor in the Division of Dance at Purdue University's School of Visual and Performing Arts.
