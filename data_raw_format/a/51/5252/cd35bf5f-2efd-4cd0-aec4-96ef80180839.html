Interests

Big Data, Visual Analytics, Visualization, Machine Learning, High Performance Computing, X-ray imaging.

Biography

Wei Xu received her Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University in 2012 and M.S. and B.S. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University. She is also a staff scientist at Computational Science Initiative of Brookhaven National Laboratory since 2013.

Research

Wei Xu’s current research is on visual analytics, visualization and deep structured learning for extreme-scale data analysis. She is also working on high performance computing on GPUs and Intel Xeon Phi coprocessors for multi-modal X-ray imaging problems.

Awards

Wei Xu was selected as Women@Energy showcase representing female scientists of STEM fields in 2014. She received the Best Paper Award in Fully3D associated High Performance Image Reconstruction workshop in 2009.
