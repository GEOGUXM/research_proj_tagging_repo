Biographical details
I am an applied statistician whose research interest is in developing high impact statistical methods and useful, easy-to-use tools for application in bioinformatics, biometrics and biology. I enjoy working in a collaborative environment with people from many different backgrounds, with an aim to enhance our knowledge and understanding of the real world data.
I am a member of the Statistics Research Group at School of Mathematics and Statistics.

Research interests
My current research interests are:
linear mixed models - in particular with application in agriculture
factor analytic models
statistical computing - in particular with speeding up parameter estimation in linear mixed models
experimental design
statistical genetics and genomics
